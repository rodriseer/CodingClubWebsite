Welcome to the 'Shady Grove Coding Club'!

We are an inclusive programming club at Shady Grove campus

The Shady Grove Coding Club is an inclusive community where students from all backgrounds, majors, and levels of coding experience are welcome!
Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or an experienced coder eager to expand your skills, our club aims to provide a supportive and collaborative environment for everyone.
Join us to explore programming, build projects, and connect with peers who share a passion for technology!
